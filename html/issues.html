Our Issues

Life

The NM Center for Family Policy is dedicated to the protection of human life from the time of conception to the end of natural life. We promote public policy to protect unborn children and their mothers. In further support of life, we oppose euthanasia, physician-assisted suicide, human cloning, and embryonic stem cell research.
