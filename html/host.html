Scott Stiegler

The popular Scott Stiegler Show runs from 4-7 pm on weekdays on 770 KKOB-AM. This news radio program discusses local and national politics and social issues plus food, music, relationships and the things that impact people's lives. Scott has been with 770 KKOB full time since 2006. Prior to that time, he was a TV sportscaster in Albuquerque for ten years. His career in radio began in Virginia in 1980.
